Full Stack Engineer - Associate
iCapital
New York, New York, United States

About the Role

iCapital is seeking an Associate Full Stack Engineer with an initial emphasis on frontend development to join the Post-Investments Technology team. This individual will work closely with multiple Engineering teams to build and evolve a TypeScript-based microservice frontend that integrates with Scala and Ruby on Rails backend systems. While the focus will start on the frontend, the role is expected to contribute across the stack over time.

Responsibilities

Design, develop, and maintain frontend applications using TypeScript, with a strong focus on usability, performance, and upkeep.
Collaborate with multiple Backend teams working in Scala and Ruby on Rails to integrate frontend services with distributed microservice architectures.
Contribute to API design and backend development as needed to support end-to-end feature delivery.
Partner with Product Managers, Designers, and other Engineering teams to translate business requirements into scalable technical solutions.
Participate in code reviews, architectural discussions, and continuous improvement of engineering practices.
Support and enhance critical, client-facing platforms and workflows.

Qualifications

Experience as a full stack engineer, with stronger depth on the frontend
Strong proficiency in TypeScript and modern frontend frameworks
Working knowledge of backend technologies such as Scala or Ruby on Rails
Experience working in a microservices-based architecture
Able to collaborate effectively across multiple teams
Experience supporting complex, high-scale, client-facing applications.
Familiar with document-heavy or workflow-driven user interfaces
Willingness to grow deeper into backend ownership over time

Benefits

The base salary range for this role is $100,000 to $135,000. iCapital offers a compensation package which includes salary, equity for all full-time employees, and an annual performance bonus. Employees also receive a comprehensive benefits package that includes an employer matched retirement plan, generously subsidized healthcare with 100% employer paid dental, vision, telemedicine, and virtual mental health counseling, parental leave, and unlimited paid time off (PTO).

We believe the best ideas and innovation happen when we are together. Employees in this role will work in the office Monday-Thursday, with the flexibility to work remotely on Friday.
