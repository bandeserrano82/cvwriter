FSA Capital LLC
Lead Software Developer (Full Stack)

Job Summary

FSA Capital is seeking a hands-on Lead Developer to support Nestly, an AI-powered home buying application developed by FSA Capital. Nestly is designed to transform the entire home purchasing journey into a single, streamlined experience. We combine institutional-grade market analytics with a certified vendor network and seamless multi-party communication to help buyers make smarter decisions, close faster, and save more.

The Lead Developer will serve as the technical backbone of the platform- responsible for the architecture, performance, and reliability of an app that guides users through the entire journey. From our AI-powered search engine and real-time pricing analytics to multi-vendor communication hubs and secure transaction workflows, you will build and scale the systems that make Nestly’s one-stop-shop vision a reality. This role is equal parts hands-on coding and technical leadership.

Key Responsibilities

Architect and maintain Nestly’s mobile application (iOS and Android) and supporting backend services, ensuring scalability from launch through rapid user growth.
